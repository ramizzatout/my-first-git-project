Here is a summary of the article:

* **Program Launch:** The Sharjah Department of Islamic Affairs has launched its 1448 AH / 2026 summer program under the theme "A Summer That Makes an Impact."
* **Participation:** Running throughout July and August, the initiative is hosting approximately 800 male and female students from across the Emirate of Sharjah.
* **Educational Objectives:** The program aims to develop character, reinforce Islamic values, and build national identity by teaching practical religious knowledge, including the pillars of Islam and the jurisprudence of prayer, fasting, and purification.
* **Leadership Appreciation:** Arif Al Shehi, Head of the Preaching Section, expressed appreciation to the UAE leadership for supporting initiatives that help youth spend their free time productively.
