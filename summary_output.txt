* **Initiative Launched:** The Sharjah Department of Islamic Affairs has launched its 1448 AH / 2026 summer programme, themed "A Summer That Makes an Impact," running throughout July and August.
* **Target Audience:** Approximately 800 male and female students from across the Emirate of Sharjah are participating in the summer initiative.
* **Core Objectives:** The programme aims to develop youth character, reinforce Islamic values, and strengthen national identity through educational activities.
* **Curriculum Focus:** Students will be taught practical religious knowledge, including the pillars of Islam and faith, as well as the jurisprudence of purification, fasting, and prayer. 
* **Leadership Acknowledgment:** Arif Al Shehi, Head of the Preaching Section, expressed gratitude to the UAE leadership for supporting educational initiatives that help young people productively use their free time.
